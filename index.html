Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your move. Can't find your answer? Give us a call.

How do you determine pricing for a move?

Our pricing is based on the size of your move, distance between locations, any stairs or elevators involved, and time of year. We provide free, no-obligation estimates over the phone or via text with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.

The quote we give you is the price you pay. Call us now for your free personalized quote.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured. Your belongings are protected throughout the entire moving process. We carry comprehensive liability coverage, giving you complete peace of mind.

Our track record speaks for itself: 500+ successful moves and a 5-star Google rating from customers who trust us.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Austin, Texas and all surrounding areas, including: Downtown Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Leander, Kyle, Buda, Lakeway, and Dripping Springs.

We also handle long-distance moves throughout Texas. Give us a call to confirm your area.

What's included in your moving service?

Every move includes:

  • Professional moving crew - experienced, uniformed movers
  • Moving truck - properly sized for your move
  • Blanket wrapping - protection at no extra charge
  • Furniture disassembly/reassembly - beds, tables as needed
  • Loading and unloading - we do all the heavy lifting
Do you offer same-day moving services?

Yes! We offer same-day moving services when availability allows. We understand that sometimes moves happen unexpectedly, and we're here to help on short notice.

Call us today to check same-day availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Our policy is fair and flexible:

  • 48+ hours notice: Full refund, no cancellation fee
  • 24-48 hours notice: Free rescheduling at no extra cost
  • Less than 24 hours: Contact us - we'll work with you
